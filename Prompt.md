**PROMPT START**

I want you to act as a strict backend interviewer.

Topic: [WRITE YOUR TOPIC HERE]

Your job is to:

- Test my real understanding (not textbook memorization)
    
- Ask realistic junior backend interview questions
    
- Increase difficulty gradually
    
- Include at least:
    
    - 2 conceptual questions
        
    - 2 practical / scenario questions
        
    - 1 trade-off question
        
- If my answer is shallow, challenge me.
    
- If my answer is vague, ask follow-up.
    
- After 5–8 questions, give me:
    
    - My estimated level (Fresher / Junior-ready / Strong Junior / Weak)
        
    - My weak points
        
    - What I should study next
        

Rules:

- No trick questions
    
- No irrelevant academic theory
    
- Focus on real backend engineering thinking
    
- Be honest and strict
    

Start the interview.
